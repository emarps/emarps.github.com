Brainstorm: Sustainability

I always enjoy brainstorming for projects because you can just let your mind wander where it wants to. For this project I decided to brainstorm topics surrounding sustainability relating to reciprocity, respect, and inclusivity. As I started writing, I began to realize that sustainability really encapsulates these iedas. Anyone no matter what socioeconomic background can practice AND gain from practicing sustainable ways of living. I am not totally sure what I am going to do for the project quite yet, but I feel like I have some ideas forming from this brainstorm.
